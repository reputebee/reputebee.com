Influence Prospects at different Stages of the Customer Journey

Reviews can influence potential customers’ decisions but different reviews are needed for different stages of the customer journey. .

Top of the funnel prospects need reviews that show it reduce time for existing customers. Middle of the funnel prospects need reviews that compare your product with others and prove its quality. Bottom of the funnel customers need reviews that demonstrate your product’s value and results. Categorize and display your reviews accordingly to provide the right social proof and increase conversions and sales.

  • Display reviews that highlight how your product saves time for customers on landing pages
  • Emphasize quality features and benefits with reviews that vouch for your product on pricing page
  • Increase conversions and sales with reviews that reveal cost-saving and service advantages of your product
